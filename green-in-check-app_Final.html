ESG Rating Lookup

Compare ESG ratings and sustainability performance metrics from multiple providers.

Why Compare Ratings?

ESG ratings can vary significantly between providers due to:

  • Different methodologies and scoring systems
  • Varying data sources and quality
  • Subjective weightings of ESG factors
Comparing multiple sources provides a more complete picture of a company's true ESG performance.

Emission Scopes

Scope 1

Scope 2

Indirect emissions from purchased electricity, steam, heating, and cooling

Scope 3

All other indirect emissions in a company's value chain (e.g., business travel, waste, purchased goods)

About the Analyzer

Our report analyzer uses advanced AI to detect potential greenwashing tactics such as:

  • Vague terminology without clear definitions
  • Selective disclosure of positive metrics
  • Hidden trade-offs between different impacts
  • Unverified claims without evidence
  • Irrelevant environmental claims
All uploads are securely processed and not stored longer than necessary for analysis.

Regulatory Compliance Checker

Verify if sustainability claims comply with regulations in specific regions and industries.

Key Regulations

EU Green Claims Directive

Requires claims to be substantiated with scientific evidence. Bans vague claims like "eco-friendly" without specifics.

FTC Green Guides (US)

Guidelines on avoiding deceptive environmental marketing claims, with specific sections on certifications, carbon offsets, and recyclability.

UK Green Claims Code

Six principles including accuracy, clarity, substantiation, and fair comparisons for environmental claims.

Compliance checks are based on current regulations and are for guidance only. They do not constitute legal advice.
